Research Projects

Funded studies, fieldwork, and collaborative initiatives in early childhood education

Active Projects

Ongoing · Funded
Teacher Recruitment & Candidacy Study

A longitudinal mixed-methods investigation examining the process and product of early childhood teacher recruitment, candidacy screening, and preparation outcomes — with a focus on underrepresented candidates at Kent State University.

Mixed Methods Teacher Prep KSU
📚
Ongoing · Field-Based
ECE Policy Reconceptualisation: Nigeria & Africa

A cross-institutional research effort examining how African philosophical frameworks, indigenous knowledge systems, and postcolonial theory can inform the reconceptualisation of early childhood education policy and research in Nigeria and across sub-Saharan Africa.

ECE Policy Nigeria Decolonial
🌍
Ongoing · Collaborative
Indigenous Language Preservation in Ungoverned Educational Spaces

Examines indigenous language acquisition and loss among primary-age pupils in communities with limited state educational infrastructure, connecting language preservation to decolonisation goals and sustainable development frameworks.

Language Decolonisation Qualitative
🏫
Completed · Published
A Tale of Two Preschools: Rural–Urban Comparative Study

Investigated disparities in preschool environmental resources — physical, material, and socio-emotional — between rural and urban communities in Ibadan, Nigeria. Findings published in peer-reviewed outlets and presented at international conferences.

Survey Design Nigeria Rural-Urban
🤝
Completed · Published
Moral Development Through Yoruba Philosophy ("Igba Oju")

A qualitative inquiry tracing indigenous Yoruba moral education practices and their relevance to whole-child development in contemporary early childhood settings in Southwestern Nigeria. Conducted via interviews with community elders.

African Philosophy Yoruba Culture Moral Dev.
📊
Completed · Doctoral Grant
Doctoral Research Grant — KSU Child Development Center

Received a doctoral research grant from Kent State University to investigate early childhood education practices and outcomes at the KSU Child Development Center, contributing to center-based policy and programming improvements.
